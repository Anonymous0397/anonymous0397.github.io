How to install Custom ROMs:

    Prerequisites:
  • A Galaxy S2 (GT-i9100 MODEL ONLY, not the Snapdragon model)
  • Odin
  • A custom recovery (See custom recoveries)
  • A custom ROM you want to install
  • A gapps flash file (Optional but recommended)
  • At least an 8GB SD Card for backing up data and having custom ROMs to install
  • And lastly, some patience
1. Check for data and back them up

Double check for any data and back them up to an SD card or tranfer it over to a computer

2.Copy the custom ROM and the gapps (if you want to flash the GAPPS) onto the SD card

Plug your S2 to the computer and make sure it's a data cable

3. Reboot your phone to download mode.

Hold down the volume down, home button and the power button

4. Flash the CWM recovery via Odin (Select AP)

MAKE SURE THAT YOU ARE FLASHING THE CORRECT RECOVERY THAT'S MEANT FOR THE ROM YOU ARE FLASHING OR ELSE IT WON'T INSTALL

5. Reboot the phone to recovery once the download mode screen disappears

If it says "SUCCESS!" on Odin, that means the recovery has been flashed successfully. Hold down the volume up + home + power button until the logo appears

6. Go to the location where you stored the custom ROM and flash the ROM you want to flash

Make sure it's not any other zip file or the gapps (yet)

7. (OPTIONAL, IF YOU HAVE IT) Flash the gapps

It MUST be made for the Android version you are flashing (e.g. Android 6.0.1)

8. Factory reset

To avoid any issues, duh

9. Reboot the phone
